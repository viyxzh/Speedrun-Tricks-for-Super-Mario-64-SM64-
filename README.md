# SM64 Speedrun Tricks

A simple `.txt` guide listing **human-performable Super Mario 64 speedrun tricks**.

This repository focuses on:
- real speedrun techniques
- no TAS-only tricks
- clear explanations of how things work
- practical inputs runners actually use

---

## Contents

- Backwards Long Jump (BLJ)
- Lakitu Skip
- Cannonless (Whomp’s Fortress)
- First-frame Wallkicks (Firsties)
- Bomb Clip
- Triple Jump Dive (TJD)

More tricks may be added over time.

---

## Format

Each trick is split into three sections:

- **Main Info** – what the trick is used for  
- **How it works** – why the trick works in-game  
- **Inputs** – how to perform it  

The goal is clarity without overcomplication.

---

## Download

To download the guide:
1. Open `SM64_SPEEDRUN_TRICKS.txt`
2. Click **Raw**
3. Press **Ctrl + S**

You can also download the entire repository as a ZIP.
